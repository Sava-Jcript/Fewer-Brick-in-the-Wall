The point of the game is to click on the right falling walls,
and there are 12 of them that include some random statements,
facts or just "CRAZY" thoughts as most Mass Media would
call them.
If you click on three "Right" answers, you win. Otherwise three
incorrect answers will end the game.
I know this Game makes no sense whatsoever. Goodluck!

Let's fight facism

This game is a project that has only one purpose: Remind people of all the inequalities and injustices that a lot of people have been facing for decades, if not centries of persecution, harassement, torture and genocides just becasue of their beliefs, color of skin, ideologies or just culinary habits that purely differ from the OTHERS.
